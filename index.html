Installing Mods

To install mods, go to the mods folder in your ".minecraft" folder, and drag your mod .jar files into it.
You can do this in a multitude of ways, first is windows specific and requires you to search: "%appdata%". There you will find your .minecraft folder.
The second option requires you to launch Minecraft with forge enabled, then press "Mods" and in that menu, press "Open mod folder". Then simply drag your .jar files into the folder.
